Yama Buddha

1987 - 2017

Yama Buddha was a talented Nepali rapper, songwriter, and music icon who revolutionized Nepali hip-hop. Through his meaningful lyrics and unique style, he inspired a generation of young artists and music lovers.

Why We Remember Him

  • Pioneer of modern Nepali rap music.
  • Inspired youth through his powerful lyrics.
  • Created timeless songs loved by millions.
  • Helped Nepali hip-hop gain national recognition.
"Legends never die. Their words and music live forever."
